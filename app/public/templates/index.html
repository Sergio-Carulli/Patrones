Instructions

  1. Open diagrams.net (web or desktop)
  2. In diagrams.net go to File > Open Library from > Device ...
  3. Select the library downloaded.
  4. Make your conceptualization using the block that will appear on the side bar.
  5. Download the diagram in xml format.
  6. Drag and drop your model in the Service dropping area and download your TTL file.
